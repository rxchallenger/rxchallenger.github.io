Career Readiness

Graduate with practical confidence. Bridge the gap between academic knowledge and professional competence.

  • Patient History

    Provides a brief, relevant medical history for each patient including chronic conditions, allergies, and other critical factors influencing prescription decisions.

  • Chief Complaints

    Summarizes the primary reasons why the patient sought medical attention, mimicking real-world scenarios where pharmacists must quickly identify primary concerns.

  • Diagnosis

    Displays the doctor's diagnosis based on patient history, symptoms, and lab results, bridging the gap between condition and prescribed medication.
