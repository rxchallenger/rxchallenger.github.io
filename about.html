About Rx Challenger

We build free, evidence-based pharmacy training tools that turn complex prescription interpretation into clinical confidence.

Rx Challenger is a mobile learning app that helps pharmacy students, recent graduates, and working pharmacists master prescription reading through gamified practice. Built by healthcare educators and software engineers under the IMC Hub ecosystem, our mission is to make high-quality pharmacy training accessible to everyone — for free.

Our mission

Every year, thousands of pharmacy graduates enter the workforce without complete confidence in reading real-world prescriptions. Abbreviations, dosage calculations, and unfamiliar drug names create real patient risk. We believe the fix is practice — short, frequent, high-yield practice sessions that fit into daily life.

Evidence-based by design

Every prescription scenario, pharmacy math question, and clinical case in Rx Challenger is created or peer-reviewed by licensed pharmacists and pharmacy educators. Content reflects current best practices, common licensure exam formats (NAPLEX, PEBC, and equivalents), and the prescriptions students will actually dispense.

Built into the IMC Hub ecosystem

Rx Challenger is one digital solution within the broader IMC Hub platform — a family of tools designed for healthcare learners. This ecosystem approach lets us share infrastructure, content quality standards, and user insights across multiple products while keeping Rx Challenger focused and specialized.

Our values

  • Access over profit. Core Rx Challenger content will always be free. We do not sell user data.
  • Clinical accuracy first. Every claim is grounded in current pharmacy references and peer-reviewed literature.
  • Build for real life. Sessions are designed for 5–10 minutes of focused practice between classes, during commutes, or between patients.
  • Transparency. Our privacy policy, terms, and cookie practices are clearly written and easy to find.
