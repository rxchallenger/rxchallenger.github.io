Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains how Rx Challenger uses cookies and similar technologies, and how you can manage your choices.

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and analyze how the site is used.

How we use cookies

Strictly necessary: Required for basic site functionality (e.g. cookie consent storage).

Analytics: Google Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. IP addresses are anonymized.

Functional: Optional cookies may remember your preferences across visits.

Your choices

You can block cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly. When you first visit, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our consent banner.

Email contact@imc-hub.github.io with any questions about cookies.
