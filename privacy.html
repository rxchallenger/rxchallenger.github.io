Privacy Policy

Last updated June 25, 2026. This policy explains how Rx Challenger handles your information.

Rx Challenger is committed to transparency. This privacy policy explains what information we collect when you use the Rx Challenger app or visit this website, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

Information we collect

Account information: When you create an account we collect an email address and password. We never sell your email address.

Learning progress: We track which questions you answer, your scores, and your streaks so the app can adapt to your level.

Device & usage: We collect anonymized device type, OS version, and app usage patterns via Google Analytics.

How we use your information

We use the information collected to: operate and improve the Rx Challenger experience; provide customer support; send service-related notifications (not marketing unless you opt in); and analyze aggregate usage patterns.

Cookies and local storage

This website uses localStorage to remember your cookie consent preference. The Rx Challenger app uses local storage to cache your progress. We use Google Analytics cookies on this website with anonymized IPs.

Third-party services

We use Google Analytics for website usage, Google Play for app distribution, and optional social media widgets (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn). These services have their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain your account and progress data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by emailing contact@imc-hub.github.io.

Your rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws you have the right to: access your data, rectify inaccuracies, request deletion, object to processing, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children's privacy

Rx Challenger is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will change. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Contact us

For privacy questions email contact@imc-hub.github.io.
