Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rx Challenger?

Rx Challenger is a gamified educational app designed to enhance the practical knowledge of pharmacy trainees and professionals. It offers real-life prescription scenarios to help users improve their drug recognition, prescription interpretation, and clinical decision-making skills.

Who is Rx Challenger for?

Rx Challenger is designed for pharmacy students in their final years, new graduates entering retail or hospital pharmacy, pharmacy managers training new hires, experienced pharmacists seeking a skills refresher, and pharmacy schools looking for practical training tools.

What platforms does Rx Challenger support?

Rx Challenger is currently available for Android (via Google Play) and Windows (direct download). An iOS version is planned for the future.

Is Rx Challenger free?

Rx Challenger is free to download and use on both Android and Windows platforms.

Is my progress saved?

Yes, Rx Challenger includes cloud-based progress saving. Log in to your account and your progress will be synced and accessible across all your devices seamlessly.

How frequently are new cases added?

New prescription cases are added monthly, ensuring you always have fresh content to continue learning.

How do the tooltips work?

Tooltips provide context-specific help as you work through each prescription case. They include patient history, chief complaints, diagnosis, and relevant lab values — giving you the same clinical context a real pharmacist would consider.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

For technical support, email us at RxChallenger.App@gmail.com with a description of the issue. We strive to respond within 48 hours.

How can I request data deletion?

Email us at RxChallenger.App@gmail.com and we will process your data deletion request promptly in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

Can Rx Challenger be used by pharmacy schools?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with pharmacy schools and training institutions. Contact us at RxChallenger.App@gmail.com to discuss institutional access.

About Rx Challenger

Developed by Ahmed Ezzat, a clinical pharmacist with a passion for improving pharmacy education. Based in Egypt, Ahmed aims to empower new graduates with practical skills to excel in their careers.
